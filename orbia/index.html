How it works

Simple rules,
deep strategy

You are the blue circle

Move using the virtual joystick. Your size is proportional to your points.

Bigger absorbs smaller

Collide with a smaller player or NPC to absorb them and gain their points.

Avoid bigger players

If a larger player touches you, you lose all your points and start over with immunity.

Viruses are different

Purple viruses cut your points in half regardless of your size. Always avoid them.
