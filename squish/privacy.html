PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2025
The short version: Stress Squish does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stress Squish ("the App"), developed and operated by Denilson Monteiro ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Stress Squish mobile application available on the Apple App Store.

By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details. All data generated by your use of the App — including your avatar customization and game settings — is stored exclusively on your local device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults).

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos or media from your device (except when you explicitly choose a background photo, which is only stored locally)
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data beyond what Apple collects through standard iOS mechanisms

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Avatar customization preferences (face, hair, outfit, name)
  • Game settings (selected background, sound preferences)
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Custom background photo (if you choose one from your gallery)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library only when you explicitly choose to set a custom background image. This permission is optional — all other features work without it.

Any photo you select is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

You can revoke this permission at any time in your iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy, which is independent of this policy. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, this policy applies equally to all age groups.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any information because we do not collect any information to share.

The share feature within the App generates a score card image on your device. You choose if and where to share it. We have no access to what you share or where.

8. Data Security

All App data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's built-in security features, including device encryption. We do not operate any servers or databases that could be compromised.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@stresssquish.app
