How do I restore my purchase?
Open Dote → tap the lock icon to open the paywall → tap "Restore Purchase" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Why aren't my habits syncing between devices?
Dote uses iCloud to sync your data. Make sure iCloud is enabled on both devices: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → toggle on for Dote. It may take a few minutes for data to appear.
How do I add the widget to my home screen?
Long press on your home screen → tap the + button in the top corner → search for "Dote" → choose a widget size → tap Add Widget. Widget requires Dote Premium.
Can I delete a habit?
Yes. Tap on a habit to open its detail view → tap the trash icon in the top right corner → confirm deletion. This action cannot be undone.
Why does the app show missed days I haven't started yet?
The calendar shows days since the habit was created. Days before your first check-in appear as missed. This is intentional — it shows your full history from day one.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your habit data is stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account. We never see, collect or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
